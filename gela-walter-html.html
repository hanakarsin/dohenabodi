Gela Walter - Age, Family, Bio

Model and popular Snapchat star who shares content about her life on her social media platforms. She is recognizable for her trademark wink to the camera. She has more than 260,000 followers on Snapchat and 40,000 followers on Instagram. 

Before Fame

The first picture posted to her Instagram account is from December 2016.
