Orla Brady - Age, Family, Bio

Irish actress best known for playing Elizabeth Bishop on the science fiction TV drama Fringe, which was produced by JJ Abrams.

Before Fame

She worked as a model and played the role of Adela in the Dublin theater production of House of Bernarda Alba.

Trivia

She appeared on the BBC TV show The Deep.

Family Life

She has three siblings. She married photographer Nick Brandt in December 2002.

Associated With

She was on an episode of the legal drama Shark alongside James Woods.
