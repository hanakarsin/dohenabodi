Ben Hull - Age, Family, Bio

Most famous for his role as Lewis Richardson on the popular British soap opera Hollyoaks, he is also notable for his starring role as Nurse Adam Fearnley on the hospital-themed soap opera The Royal Today.

Before Fame

He made his television debut in the mid-1990s, appearing in episodes of the ITV programs Revelations and Children's Ward.

Trivia

Between 2009 and 2015, he acted in nearly thirty episodes of Holby City.

Family Life

While appearing on the Channel 5 soap opera Family Affairs, he met his future wife, actress Anna Acton.

Associated With

He and Marcus Patric both rose to fame for their roles on Hollyoaks.
