Ini Edo Opens Up On Secrets That Have Made Her Successful In Nollywood

Popular Nollywood actress, Ini Edo has shared some of the attributes that have made her successful in the Nollywood industry through acting.

The screen goddess in a recent post via her Instagram account revealed that acting is not just about catching fun and enjoyment.

According to her, acting as a career requires a lot of commitment, discipline, and patience.

Ini Edo added that it can involve early morning call times and late-night work modes as well, pointing out that passion is a major ingredient that has kept her going.

She wrote: “It’s been 2 weeks of doing what I absolutely love…

“People think acting is all games and fun…it takes a high level of commitment, discipline and patience to get the job done…From very early morning call times to late night and sometimes filming into the early hours of the morning…

“The one thing that sees me through is the passion for filmmaking…This is what I was born to do….And there’s so much more coming from me to y’all…”
