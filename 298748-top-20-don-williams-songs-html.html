19. Just as Long as I Have You

Just as Long as I Have You was a song that was written by Dave Loggings, an American Singer and songwriter with the help J. D Martin. Originally, the title was recorded by Dave Loggins in 1985 with Gus Hardin. Their version was a soft hit, peaking at number 72 on the Billboard country music charts. Later, Don Williams did his version of the single that soared to number 4 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles Chart.
