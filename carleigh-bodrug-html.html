Carleigh Bodrug - Age, Family, Bio

Verified TikTok content creator and social media personality who rose to fame by sharing vegan plant-based recipes and culinary inspiration on her plantyou account. She has more than 3.2 million dedicated fans on the platform.

Before Fame

In August of 2018, she shared a quick oat and fruit meal preparation video. It was set to music by Jason Derulo.

Trivia

She encourages fans to save their orange peels in a tutorial video about making orange zest candies. It went viral with more than 1 million views. 

Family Life

She is from Canada.

Associated With

She made vegan french toast in a video set to music by rapper Soulja Boy.
