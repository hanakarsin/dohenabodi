&quot;48 Hours Apart&quot;: Twin Sisters Who Are Married to Twin Brothers Give Birth to Twin Babies Each, Phot

  • A photo has resurfaced on social media showing four babies who were delivered by twin sisters
  • The twin sisters who have separately given birth to twins are also married to twin brothers named Sa’ad and Sa’eed Yusuf
  • More stunning is the fact that the twin brothers, Sa’ad and Sa’eed Yusuf, got married to the twin sisters on the same day

A photo trending on social media shows four babies delivered by two ladies who are twins.

The four babies were separately delivered as twins just a few hours apart by the twin sisters, who also got married on the same day.

Fathers of the babies are also twins

One other thing that has stunned the public about the babies is that their fathers are also twin brothers.

The fathers of the babies are named Sa’ad and Sa’eed Yusuf, and they got married to the twin sisters, who are the mothers of the babies.

Sa’ad and Sa’eed Yusuf were pictured in a frame with their babies.

The twin babies were born on different dates, 48 hours apart

Reports circulating online said they recently gave birth and that it was on the same day, but in a chat with TheCable Lifestyle, one of the fathers said it happened in 2015 and that the babies were welcomed on separate dates.

While Sa’ad’s wife gave birth to twin girls on June 15, Sa’eed's wife delivered the boys on June 17. Both wives gave birth through caesarean section.

Sa'ad said:

“I wonder why people change the format of a story without getting in touch with the persons involved. The thing is that we’re twins, and we gave birth to twins. We’re both also married to twins. Reports that they were all born on the same date is wrong.
