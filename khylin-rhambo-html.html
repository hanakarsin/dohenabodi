Khylin Rhambo - Age, Family, Bio

Known for his feature film debut as Dink Meeker in Ender's Game, he previously landed the starring role of Charles Johnson on BET Centric's The First Family. He later booked a recurring role on Teen Wolf.

Before Fame

He began taking acting classes as early as the age of eight.

Trivia

He had guest roles on Criminal Minds and Reed Between the Lines.

Family Life

He has a younger sister named Faith.

Associated With

He played the onscreen son of Christopher Duncan on The First Family.
