Ohmwrecker - Age, Family, Bio

Also known as The Masked Gamer, this YouTuber posts content featuring a variety of games for more than 2.2 million subscribers. He is also a Twitch TV partner with more than 490,000 followers on the network. Some of the games he has played include Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout, Among Us and Minecraft. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in July of 2006, with some of his first footage coming from games such as Battlefield, Command and Conquer, Bioshock and more. 

Trivia

He won Choice Gamer at the 2017 Teen Choice Awards. He posted promotional content for Savage Jerky on his Instagram in 2017 after they sent him many packages of beef jerky. 

Family Life

His real name is Ryan Lord and he is from Chicago, Illinois. 

Associated With

He has gamed with PewDiePie, TheRPGMinx and EatMyDiction in multiplayer for the game Just Cause 2.
