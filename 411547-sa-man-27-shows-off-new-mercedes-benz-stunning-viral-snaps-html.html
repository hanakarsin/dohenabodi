Man, 27, Shows Off New Mercedes Benz in Stunning Viral Snaps

- A young man has gone viral on social media after sharing snaps from his first car purchase, a brand new Mercedes Benz

- At just 27, the new car owner has definitely reached a big milestone with the expensive purchase

- Mzansi shared their mixed reactions to the aspirational post in the comments section

A local man has taken to social media to share stunning pictures of his brand new whip. @UnamiKombanie flexed on his Twitter followers with snaps of the dazzling black car.

From his childlike smile and innocent baby face, it's difficult to believe Unami is already a grown man. In one image, the new car owner stands dressed to impress next to his ride, which has been neatly wrapped in a bright red bow.

In the next, the young man shows off his expensive watch and his hands on the wheels of his new prize, a beautiful black Mercedes Benz.
