Kat Zoe Clark - Age, Family, Bio

Australian TikTok star known for posting health and fitness content. Her videos often feature her children and updates from their lives. She has earned more than 5 million followers on her katclark account. 

Before Fame

She worked as a weight loss coach.

Trivia

One of her most popular TikToks is a summer update vlog. It has accumulated over 4 million views. In 2022, she started a podcast called Basically Besties with her daughter Latisha Clark. 

Family Life

She had Latisha when she was 17 years old. She also has a daughter named Deja. She frequently features her husband, Jonathan, on her social media. 

Associated With

She set an April 2022 TIkTok to the song "Just a Cloud Away" by Pharrell Williams.
