'We Will Not Accept Even If Peter Obi Concede Defeat'

A well-known Nigerian entertainer, Charles Oputa, better known as Charly Boy, has advised the Labour Party (LP) presidential candidate, Peter Obi against conceding defeat to President Bola Tinubu.

Naija News understands that as of now, Obi and the Labour Party are legally contesting President Tinubu’s victory.

Nonetheless, Charly Boy vowed that even if Obi chose to withdraw his complaints against President Tinubu at the Presidential Election Tribunal, he and other LP backers would not accept this outcome.

Charly Boy expressed a steadfast determination for a better Nigeria, saying it must happen “by fire by force.”

He hinted that the nation was ripe for a revolution. If the judiciary does not bring justice, he suggested that Obi’s supporters would consider other means.

Charly Boy in a post on his Twitter account on Tuesday wrote, “U know smtin? Even if Peter Okwute Obi come tell us say make we move on from the 2023 yeye selection wey Yakubu mess up, Obidients no go gree sam sam.

“I Dey tell many people say, wetin wan happen wey don overdue to happen for Nigeria pass Peter Obi.

“Naija must be better by fire by force, Obidients/ Lovers of a New Nigeria take note.

“All our eyes dey for Judiciary. If dem like make dem F*k up, we know what to do.”
