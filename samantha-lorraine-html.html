Samantha Lorraine - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who rose to fame after portraying Lydia Rodriguez Katz in the 2023 Netflix original comedy film You Are So Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah. She previously appeared as Lucy in the 2023 TV movie The Love Advisor and as Ally in the 2021 TV movie The Kid Who Only Hit Homers.

Before Fame

She launched her professional acting career in 2020, appearing in several episodes of the TV series Kid Stew, in the role of Samantha. Later that same year, she also appeared as Young Hope on several episodes of The Walking Dead: World Beyond.

Trivia

She has established a significant following on social media. She boasts over 700,000 followers on her samanthaa.lorrainee TikTok account.

Family Life

She is from Los Angeles, California. She has a dog named Petunia. 

Associated With

She co-starred with Jackie Sandler, Adam Sandler, Sadie Sandler, and Sunny Sandler in You Are So Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah.
