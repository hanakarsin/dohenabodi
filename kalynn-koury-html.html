Kalynn Koury - Age, Family, Bio

Older sister of YouNow sensation Weston Koury who has appeared alongside her brother in his broadcasts. She went on to become somewhat of a social media sensation herself, with over 290,000 followers to her kalynnkoury account. She and Weston teamed up to form the Sinjin Drowning channel in 2018. They post challenges and play a variety of games on the channel and it has amassed over 430,000 subscribers. 

Before Fame

Her first tweet, posted in April of 2014, read, "had so much fun this softball season."   

Trivia

She has posted multiple pictures with Thomas Kuc on her Instagram account. 

Family Life

She is a year and a half older than her brother Weston.

Associated With

She has been good friends with fellow social media stars Hooverr, Maya Higa, Elle Mills and Drew Phillips.
