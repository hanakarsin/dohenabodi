How to know if someone blocked you on WhatsApp in 5 ways

In this modern age, social media is the most used form of communication, and what better way to cut off communication than on WhatsApp. How do you know if someone blocked you on WhatsApp?

Ironically, the app providers have disclosed ways to know if one has been blocked. So how do you tell if someone has blocked you on WhatsApp? Here are five ways to see if you've been blocked.

1. Check the person's profile picture

The technique is one of the simplest methods to find out if you have been blocked or not. Though the contact can remove the display photo, it does not mean that one has necessarily been blocked. When someone blocks you on WhatsApp, the person's profile picture or display photo cannot be seen. Besides, updates on it are unchangeable to you.

When an individual's profile picture disappears, it means the contact has deleted the display photo or he has blocked you on the social media app.

2. Make a WhatsApp call

Another way to decipher if one has been blocked on the app is by calling the contact. Since most people on the digital app are adequately connected to the internet, WhatsApp calls have been a favourite. A peculiar fact about WhatsApp calling is the difference in the screen display from regular calls.

If the individual has blocked you, the screen will display calling rather than showing ringing. It is a fact that most WhatsApp users have not noticed. If the screen display shows ringing, the call is going through, but if it indicates calling, the contact has blocked you.

3. Look for the last seen or online timestamp

When someone blocks you on WhatsApp, you won't see the last seen or online timestamp. The method is easy to confirm if a contact has blocked you but is the least reliable. The contact number can choose to enable the setting which permanently masks their last seen timestamp. However, if this is not the case, it means the presumed contact has blocked you.

4. Send messages

This method is efficient to tell if someone has blocked you on WhatsApp. Once you have been blocked on WhatsApp, any messages sent to the number will be undelivered. Whether the turn-on receipts is on or off, the signature double tick will only display as a single tick. When you come across such, then you've been blocked on WhatsApp.

5. Trying to add or create a group with the contact

All attempts listed can be due to a simple network error or a glitch in the network being utilized. Therefore, they cannot be considered as the ultimate revelation on whether one has been blocked or not. This method can clear your doubts about being blocked.

Try to create a WhatsApp group with the contact that has presumably blocked you. When a notification says you are not authorized to add this contact, it's crystal clear that the number has blocked you.
