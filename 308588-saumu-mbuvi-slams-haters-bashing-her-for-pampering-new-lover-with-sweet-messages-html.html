Sonko's daughter Saumu Mbuvi slams critics bashing her for showering Lamu senator with sweet message

It is now evident that Nairobi county governor Mike Sonko's first born daughter Saumu Mbuvi has been smitten by Lamu Senator Anwar Loitiptip and their relationship secret is all out now.

She has been showering the legislator with love dovey messages ad this seems to have attracted haters.

Saumu recently went head to head with some of her critics who faulted her for sharing photo of her latest lover and gushing over him just days after he was attacked while trying to protect her.

In an Instagram post seen by TUKO.co.ke, Saumu thanked her new lover Anwar for protecting her and wished him a quick recovery even as he recuperates.

''Hi Future,Thank you for putting your life before mine, for standing by me, advising me and always being there for me, you will forever be my best friend. Wishing you quick recovery hun,'' the single mother of one gushed.

“I need that grace of moving on like the ex nigga never existed. But you are too much. Keep your relationships off social media. Pole senator and congrats for defending your fourth wife to be," one random fan posed.
