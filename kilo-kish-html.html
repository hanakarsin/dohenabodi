Kilo Kish - Age, Family, Bio

Artist and entertainer who has worked as a painter, fashion designer, and model/actress. She is best known for her musical releases, including 2016's studio album "Reflections in Real Time." She released an ablum titled "AMERICAN GURL" in 2022.

Before Fame

She first began her musical pursuit when she started a project called the Kool Kats Klub.

Trivia

She has released solo efforts including Homeschool EP, K+, and Across. Some of her musical inspirations include Deftones and Lenny Kravitz.

Family Life

She was born in Orlando and lived in New Jersey for a time before returning to Orlando.

Associated With

She has collaborated with Childish Gambino, the stage name of Donald Glover.
