Trinity Trinity and Beyond - Age, Family, Bio

Comedy, challenge, prank and vlog YouTubing sensation notable for making up one-half of the channel Trinity and Beyond alongside her younger sister Madison. The sisters starred in a series of videos titled "I Mailed Myself...." which earned millions of views each. The duo are popular their trinityandbeyond.yt Instagram account where they share photos of their toys and family.

Before Fame

The channel was launched in October of 2016 and kicked off with a number of "Elf on the Shelf" videos.

Trivia

"World's Most Deadly Fidget Spinner! Dangerous Tricks Gone Horribly Wrong 1000 MPH + Free Giveaway" was the first video on the channel to be watched 10 million times. Videos such as this helped the channel reach 6 million subscribers.

Family Life

Her parents often appear in her videos. In 2019, her younger brother Preston was born.
