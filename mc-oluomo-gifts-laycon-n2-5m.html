MC Oluomo Gifts Laycon N2.5m

Musiliu Akinsanya popularly known as MC Oluomo has gifted the BBNaija Lockdown winner, Laycon, a sum of 2.5 million naira.

The president of the Nigerian Union of Road Transport Workers, NURTW, Lagos chapter welcomes Laycon at his Oshodi residence on Thursdays, Oct. 7, 2020.MC Oluomo, shared photos of the event on his social media page. He wrote;

“It was a merry moment on Thursdays, Oct. 7, 2020, as Alhaji Musiliu Akinsanya hosted the BBN lockdown winner -Laycon- and a team of youths.

“The evening was marked with pump, funfair and an array of goodwill message for and from both icons (Laycon and MC Oluomo).

“As visitation winded down, Hon. Musa Babatunde Akanni(MAKA) who was the brain behind the evening asked Laycon and other youths present to join him in presenting an award of excellence to the state chairman of transport workers Alhaji Musiliu Ayinde Akinsanya (MC Oluomo) for his outstanding support and contributions towards the development of the youths in Lagos State.”

Meanwhile in another development Laycon, has spoken out about the ongoing End SARS protest going on across the nation.

Laycon, while declaring his support for the protesters, prayed “God bless everyone protesting and everyone lending a voice.”

On its Twitter handle Saturday, Laycon said the will of the people is the will of the state.

He said, “You don’t have to go through it to speak about it or make a change, you just gotta give a damn!

“God bless everyone protesting and everyone lending a voice in one way or another. The will of the people is the will of the State.
