Queen KhaMyra - Age, Family, Bio

Actor and model whose gained a strong following on social media due to her many talents. She has over 1.5 million followers on Instagram and her YouTube channel has racked up over 24 million views. In 2021, she began hosting a talk show called Tea With The Queen on Facebook Watch.

Before Fame

She began posting on Instagram in February 2018 and on TikTok in August 2018.              Queen KhaMyra just made the 2022 Forbes 30 under 30 List, at just 15 years old!

Trivia

She's been featured on the Rebel Teens Instagram page and has walked the runway for various teen brands like Angel Joi and Love Pink Corp. She was named as one of the models for Young Socialites Clothier at New York Fashion Week 2019. In 2022, she was named to the Forbes 30 Under 30 list at the age of 15. 

Family Life

Her mother manages her social media accounts. Her older brother was featured in her November 2020 video titled, "Who's Most Likely Too (Sibling Edition)." She has lived in Atlanta. 

Associated With

She is a member of Collab Crib alongside Tray Bills.
