Freddie Thorp - Age, Family, Bio

Earning his most notable role as Garrett Foster in 2017's Overdrive, he is an actor also known for performances in 2016 films titled To Dream and The Head Hunter. In 2021, he appeared as Riven in the Netflix series Fate: The Winx Saga.

Before Fame

He was a student at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

Trivia

He made his first TV appearance in an episode of Doctors in 2015, and then appeared in a TV movie called The Advocate.

Family Life

His parents are named Nick and Antonia Thorp.

Associated With

He starred alongside Scott Eastwood in Overdrive.
