Valentino Rossi - Age, Family, Bio

Dominant Italian MotoGP rider who won his seventh MotoGP World Championship in 2009. He also won a 125cc World Championship in 1997 and a 250cc World Championship in 1999.

Before Fame

He won his first go-kart racing championship at age 11.

Trivia

He broke an international record by making 170 consecutive starts between 2000 and 2010.

Family Life

He had a relationship with Mandala Tayde in 2008. He later began dating model Francesca Novello. They had a child, Giulietta Rossi, in March 2022. 

Associated With

He won two consecutive MotoGP titles before Jorge Lorenzo won the 2010 championship.
