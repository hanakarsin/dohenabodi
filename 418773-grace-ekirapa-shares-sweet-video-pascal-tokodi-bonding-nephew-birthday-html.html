Grace Ekirapa Shares Sweet Video of Pascal Tokodi Bonding with Nephew on His Birthday

  • Grace Ekirapa shared an adorable video of hubby Pascal Tokodi and their nephew, Jamie, on the young lad’s birthday
  • Tokodi playfully smeared cake on Jamie’s face while imitating the Lion King’s soundtrack
  • The video proved Tokodi is a cool uncle to Jamie, the son of Grace’s sister Ashlyne

On July 13, 2021, media personality Grace Ekirapa posted an adorable video of Pascal Tokodi bonding with his nephew as the young lad turned 10.

In the video, Tokodi was seen smearing cake on the face of Jamie, who is the son of Grace's sister Ashlyne Ekirapa.

The couple accompanied Ashlyne to Jamie’s school to cut a cake to celebrate his special day.

Tokodi smeared the cake on Jamie's face while imitating The Lion King soundtrack, eliciting laughter from his classmates.
