Missing Schoolgirls: Dramatic Photos Of Chibok Girls Who Escaped From Boko Haram

It's been over three weeks since more than 200 girls were abducted by Boko Haram from Government Girls’ Secondary School, in Chibok, Borno and they yet to be released.

Some of the girls who escaped

The abduction of the girls has raised a lot of controversies as some people have been asking for the names of those who were kidnapped and photos of those who escaped. Some, like Asari Dokubo have even insinuated that it's all a scam and no girl was kidnapped.

Also the number of girls who were kidnapped is controversial. At first it was 234, later 276 and then 300.c

However, photographs of the girls who managed to escape have surfaced. The photos which were taken in front of the school, with the remains of the burned out building seen in the background, tells a dramatic story of a tiny, and isolated village, Chibok, that the world has now come to know as the site of a mass and disturbing kidnapping.

The girls who escaped & their mothers

Woman weeping profusely

The girls who managed to escape from the insurgents can be seen with their mothers/relatives, weeping profusely as they recount their horrible experience during the night raid on April 14, 2014 to the public.
