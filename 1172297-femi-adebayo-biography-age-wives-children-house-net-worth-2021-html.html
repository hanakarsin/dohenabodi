Femi Adebayo biography: age, wives, children, house &amp; net worth

Femi Adebayo is a renowned Nigerian actor, director, movie producer, lawyer, entrepreneur and politician. He is also the Special Adviser to the Governor of Kwara State on Arts, Culture and Tourism. Femi is the son of veteran Nigerian actor Adebayo Salami.

The actor is known for his gifted, energetic and highly influential nature in the Nigerian film industry. He is popular for his role in part one and two of the hilarious Yoruba comedy movie, Jelili. He has also starred in Atona, Ladies Gang and Omo Pupa.

Femi Adebayo's biography

The actor was born on 31st December 1972 in Lagos, Nigeria. How old is Femi Adebayo? He is 48 years old as of 2021. Femi is the son of legendary actor Adebayo Salami, who is popularly known as Oga Bello, and Alhaja Risikat Adebayo.

He was raised in a polygamous family, and he has 12 siblings. Some of his siblings known to the public are Tope, Sodiq, Kemi, Lai, and Omolara Adebayo.

Education

The actor studied at the C&S College for his secondary school education before joining the university. He then enrolled at the University of Ilorin, where he pursued a Bachelor's Degree in Law. He proceeded to the University of Ibadan for a Master's Degree in Law.

Career

Femi began his career as an actor in 1985. He starred in his father's film, Ogun Ajaye, while he was 13 years old. He has starred in more than 200 Nollywood movies to-date.
