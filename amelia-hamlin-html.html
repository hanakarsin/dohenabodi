Amelia Gray Hamlin - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram sensation who rose to fame as the daughter of renowned actors Harry Hamlin and Lisa Rinna. She has over 1.2 million followers on her ameliagray Instagram account.  

Before Fame

She made her first television appearance in a 2010 episode of Harry Loves Lisa.

Trivia

She made regular appearances on the children's television series Rachel & the TreeSchoolers.

Family Life

Her elder sister Delilah is also incredibly popular on Instagram. Her elder brother Dimitri is an actor and model born from her father and actress Lisa Rinna. From 2020 to 2021, she dated Scott Disick. 

Associated With

She is friends with model and Instagram sensation Kaia Gerber.
