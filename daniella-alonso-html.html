Daniella Alonso - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who played Nora Clayton on the NBC series, Revolution, from 2012 to 2013. She also starred in Wrong Turn 2: Dead End. She was discovered by Ford Modeling Agency at the age of 15.

Before Fame

She began her career by modeling for Seventeen and YM and appearing in commercials for Cover Girl and Clairol.

Trivia

Her television credits include episodes of Law & Order, Saving Grace, and Without a Trace. She appeared in ten episodes of the television series Friday Night Lights, as Carlotta Alonso.

Family Life

She was born in New York City to a Peruvian father and a Puerto Rican mother. She grew up with a brother and a sister.

Associated With

She had a recurring role on One Tree Hill, a CW series starring Chad Michael Murray.
