Michaela Conlin - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who played the free-spirited Angela Montenegro on the TV series Bones. She was also casted for a role in the film, Bad Trip.

Before Fame

She went to Amsterdam to study with the Experimental Theater Wing's International Training Program. Following her graduation from NYU, She was chosen to be a part of the cable documentary series The It Factor.

Trivia

She played May in the 2007 film Enchanted. She also play part in the TV series Dollface as Delphine.

Family Life

She was born to Fran and Denise Conlin. Her father is Irish American. She has an older sister.

Associated With

She has been good friends with co-star Emily Deschanel.
