Megan Lee (Pop Singer) - Age, Family, Bio

K-pop singer who rose to fame through her many YouTube cover videos of artists like Kelly Clarkson. In May 2014, she released her debut single, "8dayz" and in 2015, she was cast as Sun Hi in the Nickelodeon series Make It Pop.

Before Fame

She got her start acting in commercials for companies like Verizon and on programs like Kidz Bop at age ten.

Trivia

She won the Best Speech Contest at the Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards in 2008. 

Family Life

She persuaded her parents to allow to her to travel to South Korea to study K-Pop. She has two younger siblings.

Associated With

One of her YouTube covers which earned a large number of hits was Christina Aguilera's "I Will Be".
