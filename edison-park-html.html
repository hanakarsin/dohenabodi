Edison Park - Age, Family, Bio

World champion speed cuber best known for his Twitch live channel where he streams gameplay and "in real life" content. He talks about relationships, college, industry, tech, and life in general.

Before Fame

He is a former techie who worked for Microsoft. He left his engineering role to pursue his dreams.

Trivia

He is part of collaborative groups known as Just Friends and Offline TV. He attended UC Berkeley.

Family Life

He is from Los Angeles, California. He began dating Fuslie towards the beginning of 2016. They became engaged in April 2019 but broke it off in October 2021. 

Associated With

He is a collaborator of fellow Twitch star and Offline TV personality Fedmyster.
