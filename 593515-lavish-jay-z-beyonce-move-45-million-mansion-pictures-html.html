Lavish! Jay Z And Beyonce Move Into $45 Million Mansion (PICTURES)

Jay Z and Beyonce have moved into a $45 million apartment after being told to vacate the house they previously occupied.

[article_adwert]

Actually, the power couple took a 1-year lease on the new property which is in the range of $150, 000 a month according to TMZ.

The 20, 000 square feet property is grand and comes with an indoor Olympic-sized swimming pool, an outdoor pool with waterfalls and lush gardens.

Daily Mail describes the mansion as having ‘living areas that boast floor-to-wall windows, white marble floors, a gourmet kitchen, spa as well as a steam room’.

TMZ says the music heavyweights have a house in New York but chose to relocate to Los Angeles citing reasons that it is a more conducive environment to raise daughter, Blue Ivy Carter.

The power couple move into their new 5-bedroom mansion owned by former LA Dodgers owner Frank McCourt.
