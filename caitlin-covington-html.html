Caitlin Covington - Age, Family, Bio

Known as the founder of the beauty and lifestyle blog Southern Curls & Pearls. She gained popularity there for documenting her daily life and love of fashion, beauty, and fitness. She posts travel, wellness, and lifestyle content on her popular cmcoving Instagram page. 

Before Fame

She graduated with honors from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with her Bachelor of Arts degree in journalism. She began regularly blogging in April 2011.

Trivia

In October 2012, she was hired as a public relations consultant for Crawford Strategy. She has also developed a sizable Instagram following due to her blog's success with more than 1.3 million followers.

Family Life

She was born and raised in Charlotte, North Carolina alongside her brother. Her mother takes most of her Instagram photos. In May 2017, she became engaged to Chris Dorsch. She has two children, Kennedy and Collins. 

Associated With

She set one of her Christmas cookie videos on Instagram to the song "Rudolph The Red-Nosed Reindeer" by Gene Autry.
