Jason Derulo and His Baby Mama Revealed Their Baby Boy on Halloween

Jason Derulo and his baby mama split months after welcoming their son. After they revealed the infant on Halloween, fans think they may reconcile.

Pop singer Jason Derulo has become a permanent fixture in the headlines. From social media users clowning him about his impromptu dance videos to the tales of his love life, the star has always been a hot topic. And since he welcomed his first child, social media users have become more intrigued with his life.

Many fans remember the days of Jason dating Jordin Sparks and even considering the idea of marriage. Those days are long behind us as the singer is now a first-time father to a baby son, Jason King, whom he shares with model Jena Frumes. The pair recently revealed their little one to the world on Halloween, and now fans believe that they may reconcile. So, are Jason Derulo and his baby mama getting back together? Here’s the 4-1-1.

Jason Derulo and baby mama Jena Frumes welcomed their baby boy in May 2021.

In case you’ve been MIA, Jason and Jena welcomed their first child, Jason King, on May 8, 2021, per People. The duo, who dated from March 2020 to September 2021, shocked fans with their breakup.

For starters, Jason and Jena split a mere four months after having baby Jason. Not to mention, the “Ridin' Solo” singer was first to share the news on social media — which many fans side-eyed online.

"Jena and I have decided to part ways," Jason wrote on Twitter. "She is an amazing mother but we feel being apart at this time will allow us to be the best versions of ourselves and the best parents we could be."

People reports that Jena posted a sweet birthday tribute to Jason, just a day before the breakup was announced. Making matters worse, they both share the same birthday. Yikes!

“Blessed to share the same day of birth with my lover,” Jena wrote in a now-deleted post. "You are the most handsome, hardworking, talented, silly, loving human ever. You truly make me whole and I'm so grateful for the love we share."

She continued, "You and our mini make me the happiest girl in the world and I can't wait to make more memories with you guys. I know I'm a tough cookie but you make me soft and accept me for who I am and I'm forever grateful for that. Cheers to another year! I love you so much, forever.”

Are Jason Derulo and Jena Frumes back together? Fans are convinced.

Word on the Hollywood streets is that Jason and Jena may be giving their coupledom another shot. According to E!, the pair was spotted on Oct. 17, 2021, grabbing lunch together — without baby Jason in tow.

Keep in mind, this is just a few days after the pair were spotted together with their little one out and about in West Hollywood on Oct. 11, 2021.

Plus, the infamous breakup-announcement tweet from Jason’s Twitter account has been deleted. So, fans are convinced that they’re quietly trying to work things out.

Jason and Jena also made it a point to participate in the Halloween festivities for baby Jason King’s first celebration, On Nov. 1, 2021, Jen shared a video of the trio all dressed up in matching Superman costumes.

While the couple once decided to keep the baby boy’s face off social media, it appears that they’ve had a change of heart. The adorable boy can be seen laughing it up with the pair as they toss him back and forth. Fans and fellow celebrities alike gushed about how adorable baby Jason is.

"OMG he is cute! OMG," model Lira Galore commented.

"Ohhh my goodness Jena! God bless him! Such a handsome little man," a fan commented.

As of now, neither Jason nor Jena have confirmed or denied the rumors of reconciliation. While it would be great to see the pair make their relationship work, being great co-parents to baby Jason is of the utmost importance.
