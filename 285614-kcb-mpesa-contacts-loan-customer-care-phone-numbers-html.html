KCB M-Pesa customer care contacts

KCB M-Pesa customer care strives to offer instant help to its customers around the clock. As a result, they have several contact numbers where you can reach out to them. Depending on the gravity of your problem, you can also visit their head offices in Nairobi. Here is how you can contact KCB M-Pesa customer care:

Phone, fax, and SMS numbers

Currently, you can contact them using around six numbers. Most of them are the same as those used to call KCB customer care. In other words, if you are a Kenya Commercial Bank customer, there are high chances that you may have come across some of them. They include:

  • Telephone: +254 (20) 3270000
  • Mobile phone 1: 0711 087000
  • Mobile phone 2: 0732 187000
  • WhatsApp number: +254711087087
  • Fax 1: +254 732 187001
  • Fax 2: +254 711 087001

If any of the KCB M-Pesa customer care numbers does not work, you can always try another number on the list. Alternatively, you can contact them on social media. Often, they reply within a few minutes or an hour at maximum.
