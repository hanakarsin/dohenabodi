Elizabeth Ruiz - Age, Family, Bio

Model and Instagram sensation who has amassed more than 500,000 followers on her elizabethruizxo account. She's best known for her funny videos, including favorites such as "How Girls Jump to Conclusions" and "Dreams vs Reality."

Before Fame

She started posting to Instagram in January of 2012 while working as a waitress at Hooters.

Trivia

She's appeared in music videos with Snoop Dogg, Trey Songz and Nicki Minaj.

Family Life

She was born in Sagua la Grande, Cuba. She was romantically linked to football player Von Miller in 2016.

Associated With
