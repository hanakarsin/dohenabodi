4G Capital products

So far this company has had a good track record in giving entrepreneurs access to quick and unsecured loans. The company has done so through its product called Kuza by 4G Capital.

How does Kuza work?

Using Kuza is easy. The best part about it is that you do not need any security to qualify for a 4G Capital loan through Kuza. Here are the steps to follow.

  • As a vendor, you request for credit when you want to buy goods.
  • 4G Capital confirms the credit amount and terms, and you will get a confirmation SMS.
  • The delivery agent also receives a confirmation message and delivers the goods.
  • The credit details are recorded in the partner system.
  • After selling your goods at a profit, you repay the loan and qualify for repeat loans.

However, before you follow the listed steps, you should register with 4G Capital by contacting them using the details highlighted below. Upon successful registration, you will need your credentials when you visit the 4G Capital login page.

Smart Duka

This is another essential product that goes hand-in-hand with Kuza. Smart Duka is a solution provided by TechnoServe, which is one of 4G Capital’s partners. The solution aims at educating micro-retail owners on how to use credit to increase profit margins.

UPIA

UPIA is the latest product of this growing fintech. This product is for any business owner who wants to grow their business. You do not need any guarantors or assets to access an unsecured loan. UPIA is a word that the company coined from the first letters of the words; Unsecured, Private, Instant, and Affordable.
