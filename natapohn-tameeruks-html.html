Natapohn Tameeruks - Age, Family, Bio

Notable for her Focus Award and Golden Television Award-winning work in Khun Chai Rachanon, this Thai actress is also well known for her starring roles in the Channel 3 shows Dong Poo Dee, Phu Pha Prai Mai, and Tawan Chai Nai Marn Mek.

Before Fame

Early in her career, she played the lead role in the television drama Naruk.

Trivia

She developed a close friendship with fellow actress Margie Rasri Balenciaga.

Family Life

She and her siblings Pin and Tao grew up in Thailand in a family of both Thai and Chinese heritage.

Associated With

She and fellow Thai actor Mario Maurer were both in the cast of the 2014 movies The Rising Sun: Roy Ruk Hak Liam Tawan and The Rising Sun: Roy Fun Tawan Duerd.
