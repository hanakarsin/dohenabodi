Confusion As Big Plane Is Spotted On Ikeja Road

Lagos State residents were left in awe on Tuesday night after spotting a big plane on Agege-Ikeja motor road.

The uncommon development has caused heavy traffic on the road as motorists could not pass through the route at the time, Naija News reports.

Details about the rare event is still sketchy at the moment but there are speculations that the plane had suffered a mishap on Tuesday night.

Videos shared on social media showed the plane causing miles-long traffic congestion.

Naija News understands that the airplane which did not appear marked became a center of attraction today on the Agege Motor Road near the busy Ikeja Along Bus Stop.

Reports suggest that the plane allegedly crash-landed around 8:33 p.m. while some others said it was being towed to a repair workshop near the airport.
