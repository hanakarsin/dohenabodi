Zaz - Age, Family, Bio

French soul singer most famous for her 2010 hit single "Je Veux." Her self-titled debut album was a huge international smash, earning Diamond status in her native France.

Before Fame

She, her brother, and her sister were all enrolled in the Conservatoire de Tours, where he studied from ages five to eleven.

Trivia

A "L'Internaute" survey named her as the most popular French singer of 2010.

Family Life

Her mother taught Spanish while her father was an electric company employee. She began dating Chris Zylka in 2013. 

Associated With

She and Netherlands singer Caro Emerald have both received the European Border Breakers Award.
