Richerry - Age, Family, Bio

Gamer best known for his iTemp Plays YouTube channel where he posts content on games such as Call of Duty and Battlefield 1 for over 1 million subscribers. He also has a popular Roblox YouTube channel called Richicken. 

Before Fame

He launched his iTemp YouTube channel in 2012 and posted his first video, "BO2: SnD on Hijacked :: Machinima Contract Pay-cut Misinterpretations," in December 2012. 

Trivia

His most-watched video of 2018 on his Richicken channel was "I was Babysitting Olive and Poppy But They... - Roblox Bloxburg Roleplay."

Family Life

He began dating fellow Roblox gamer Amberry.

Associated With

Through his relationship with Amberry, he became acquainted with her sister Phoeberry.
