Sy Kravitz' biography

Sy, known fully as Seymour Kravitz, was born on December 10, 1924, in Brooklyn, New York City. His parents are Joseph Kravitz and Jean Kaufman Kravitz, although not much information about them is public. However, they were Jewish-Russian immigrants, so Sy has mixed ethnicity.

Seymour grew up alongside a younger brother, Leonard Harold Kravitz, with whom he shared an intimate relationship. Unfortunately, he died at 19 years old while fighting in the Korean war while defending the country against a Chinese attack.

Losing his brother affected Sy immensely, mainly because some family members blamed him for Leonard's death. The reason for these claims was that Leonard had taken after his big brother in joining the military. In his death, the government awarded Sy's brother the Distinguished Service Cross.
