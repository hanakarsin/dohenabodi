Anna Kournikova - Age, Family, Bio

Former tennis player who ranked as high as #8 in the world. She reached the semi-finals at Wimbledon in 1997. 

Before Fame

She received her first tennis racquet as a New Year's gift.

Trivia

She was featured in the 2004 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue and she was a trainer on season 12 of The Biggest Loser.

Family Life

In December 2017, she welcomed twins named Nicholas and Lucy with her longterm partner Enrique Iglesias. In January 2020, she and Iglesias welcomed another daughter named Mary. She was previously married to Sergei Fedorov. 

Associated With

She was a longtime doubles partner of Martina Hingis.
