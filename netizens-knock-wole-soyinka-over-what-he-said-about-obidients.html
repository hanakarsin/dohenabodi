Netizens Knock Wole Soyinka Over What He Said About Obidients'

Netizens have knocked Nobel laureate, Prof. Wole Soyinka over his comment about supporters of the Labour Party’s presidential candidate, who are popularly known as ‘Obidients.’

Naija News recalls that Soyinka on Tuesday had in a statement titled “Media responsibility,’’ revealed how he advised the Labour Party flag bearer, Peter Obi, about how the “excesses” of ‘Obidients’ would affect his fate at the polls.

The Nobel laureate in the statement had said “My rejection of fascism is nothing new. On three occasions, I was able to send a message to Peter Obi that if he lost the election, it would be his followers who lost it for him.

“It was depressing to watch his lieutenant, a crucially positioned voice of a movement that has ‘broken the mould’, threaten the totality of social existence.”

However, some ‘Obidients’ were not happy about his comment and they have taken to the microblogging platform, Twitter to react.
