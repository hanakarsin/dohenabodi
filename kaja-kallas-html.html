Kaja Kallas - Age, Family, Bio

Prime Minister of Estonia who entered office in January 2021 after the resignation of Jüri Ratas. She has also served as the leader of the nation's Reform Party since 2018. She is mostly active on Twitter, where she has amassed over 230,000 followers.

Before Fame

Before her political career, she worked as an attorney specializing in European and Estonian competition law.

Trivia

She is the first female Prime Minister in the history of Estonia. Her great-grandfather Eduard Alver is one of the founders of the Republic of Estonia.

Family Life

Her father was Siim Kallas, the 14th Prime Minister of Estonia. She had one son with a previous partner, and married her husband Arvo in 2018.

Associated With

She met with Finnish Prime Minister Sanna Mirella Marin in 2021.
