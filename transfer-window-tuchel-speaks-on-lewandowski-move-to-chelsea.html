Tuchel Speaks On Lewandowski Move To Chelsea

Chelsea manager, Thomas Tuchel has reacted to viral reports linking the Blues with signing Bayern Munich’s Robert Lewandowski.

Chelsea had just signed Raheem Sterling from Manchester City to boost its frontline in the new season.

Speculations are that the Blues are also interested in signing Lewandowski and Manchester United star, Cristiano Ronaldo to add to the squad before the transfer window closes.

Speaking at an interview following Sterling’s arrival at the Stamford Bridge yesterday, Tuchel said he couldn’t rule out the probability of signing another player but that signing another forward is not the priority at the moment.

The German tactician said his focus is on Chelsea’s backline considering that the likes of Antonio Rudiger and Andreas Christensen are out of his list.

“I would not rule it out [signing another striker] but right now it is not the priority. The priority right now is defence, it’s not a secret. From there, we need to see what’s possible.

“We’re competing against the two top teams, but Tottenham did a very active and aggressive and early transfer period, they have maybe the strongest squad they ever had with one of the best coaches in the world.

“Man Utd are never shy of recruiting with a new coach and Arsenal had some big signings already, so we need to keep the level up to be competitive,” Tuchel said.
