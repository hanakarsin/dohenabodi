Sage Mellet - Age, Family, Bio

Younger sister of internet sensation and movie actor Troye Sivan. She is also the elder sister of YouTube phenomenon of Tyde Levi. She has over 150,000 followers on her sageybabey Instagram account. 

Before Fame

She was born in Australia only a couple of months after the Sivan family moved from South Africa.

Trivia

She was featured in Tyde's sibling tag YouTube video.

Family Life

She was born to Laurelle and Shaun Mellet. Her father was raised Jewish and her mother converted to Judaism. She has another brother named Steele.

Associated With

She met John Cleese when Troye worked on the film Spud.
