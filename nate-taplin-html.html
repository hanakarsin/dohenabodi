Nate Taplin - Age, Family, Bio

Australian TikTok star who is known for being one half of the creative team Shay and Nate. He is known for documenting his life through TikTok including travels and dances which he shares with his over 140,000 fans. He has shared his travel content on his nathanieltaplin Instagram account as well.

Before Fame

He trained to become a professional dancer. He first went viral after a Best Friends Check video earned him over 230,000 likes and a ForYou feature. 

Trivia

He is also a model and ambassador for several brands such as BooHoo Man and Cotton On. He has gained over 50,000 followers on his Instagram account.

Family Life

He was born on the north island of New Zealand and moved to Gold Coast, Australia in his early childhood.  

Associated With

He has created viral videos to the music of E-40, Sabrina Carpenter and several other artists.
