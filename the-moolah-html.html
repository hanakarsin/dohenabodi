Aug 4, 2024 Lourie Helzer

The Fabulous Moolah - Trivia, Family, Bio

Late professional wrestler known for being the first-ever WWF Women's Champion. She was also an NWA World Women's Champion, holding the title for almost thirty years.

Before Fame

She was briefly married at age fourteen and got her start wrestling for famed women's wrestling promoter Billy Wolfe.

Trivia

In 1999, at age 76, she became the oldest WWF Women's Champion in history.

Family Life

Her birth name is Mary Lillian Ellison. She was married to Walter Carroll and wrestler Buddy Lee.

Associated With

She was longtime friends with fellow women's wrestler Mae Young.
