Ziplok - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper, hip-hop producer, and entrepreneur best known for singles such as "Cigerillo" and "Rap Fame." He boasts more than 1 million followers on his ziplok_ Instagram account. He has also released singles "Excuse Me" and "Ur A Hata," the music videos for which were both produced by BangOut.

Before Fame

He posted his first YouTube video in March 2007. The video features him walking through snowy woods and talking to the viewer. A month later, he posted a video to the channel of his friend doing a freestyle rap.  

Trivia

He owns and operates the Florida-based recording facility Ziplok Studio. He also launched his own record label called Marijuana Records. 

Family Life

He was born in New Hampshire.

Associated With

Both he and fellow performer Mandy Moore were born in New Hampshire.
