Dez Bryant - Age, Family, Bio

Wide receiver drafted 24th overall by the Dallas Cowboys in the 2010 NFL Draft. He quickly became one of the league's top receivers, racking up 1,382 yards and 12 touchdowns in just his third season. 

Before Fame

He was a dominant wide receiver for the Oklahoma State Cowboys, however he was ruled ineligible midway through the 2009 season for violating an NCAA bylaw. 

Trivia

He was born to a teenage mother and she was arrested for selling crack cocaine when he was just 8.

Family Life

He got into an altercation with his biological mother which made headlines, though charges were never pressed against him. His first son Zayne was born in 2007 and his second son Dez Jr. came three years later.

Associated With

He became one of the favorite targets of quarterback Tony Romo in Dallas.
