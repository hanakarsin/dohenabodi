Opinion Space Kenya survey panel review: is it worth joining?

Are you thinking of venturing into online surveys? Perhaps a friend was talking about it, and it sparked some interest in you? Paid surveys are the easiest way to make money online, and Opinion Space Kenya is one of the popular websites. But is it worth your time?

Opinion Space Kenya creates polls and surveys online where potential consumers can give genuine feedback about a particular product or service. It was developed in UC Berkely and first used in 4th April 2009, to find out how people felt over the legalization of marijuana. The survey site has since spread to the rest of the world. Brands are now using it to get to know what consumers think about their products and services.

Opinion Space Kenya registration process

The Opinion Space registration process involves signing up and verifying your profile through your email. Before you can participate in any survey, there are various categories of personal questions that you are required to answer, ranging from healthcare, research, mother and baby, region, food and beverage, hobbies, and travel, to name a few. Each category has a different number of questions.

How to earn and their payment options

The process is similar to what goes on in other survey sites such as Triaba, Paid Surveys, and iPoll sign up page. First, you have to be within the covered areas to sign up. After Opinion Space Kenya log in, you will receive surveys in your email whenever available. Profiles with a 100% completion rate stand higher chances of getting surveys.
