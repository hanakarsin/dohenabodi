Justina Blakeney - Age, Family, Bio

Non-fiction author of the New York Times Best-Selling book The New Bohemians. She runs a blog called The Jungalow which creates further inspiration from the themes of her book. 

Before Fame

Her first book 99 Ways to Cut, Sew, Trim & Tie Your T-shirt Into Something Special was published in 2006. 

Trivia

She has been considered a top Instagram designer to follow by Harper's Bazaar, NY Mag, and Lonny Magazine. She has over 500,000 followers on her justinablakeney Instagram account.  

Family Life

Her husband is named Jason. They have a daughter named Ida and a cat named Luda. 

Associated With

Julie Sarinana is another lifestyle blogger.
