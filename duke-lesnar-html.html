Duke Lesnar - Age, Family, Bio

Most well known as the son of wrestlers Brock Lesnar and Rena Sable Mero. 

Before Fame

His parents were married in 2006. His father had previously been in a relationship with Nicole McClain. 

Trivia

He has lived with his parents in both Minnesota and Saskatchewan. 

Family Life

He has one brother, Turk, and two half-siblings, Mya Lynn and Brock Jr. 

Associated With

His father has faced off against wrestlers like Randy Couture and Heath Herring.
