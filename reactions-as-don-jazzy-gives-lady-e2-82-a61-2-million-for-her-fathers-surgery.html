Reactions As Don Jazzy Gives Lady 1.2 Million For Her Fathers Surgery

Popular producer and singer, Don Jazzy has stirred reaction after giving a lady 1.2 million Naira for her father’s surgery after their family could not raise the required amount.

The mavin record boss donated the 1.2 million Naira balance required for the surgery after the lady took to social media to plead for donations.

The lady identified as @Hillybaby4 had taken to her Twitter account to post a photo of her ailing father, adding that they needed 2 million Naira for his surgery.

It was gathered that the lady and her sibling had tagged a lot of celebrities on Instagram, but no help was offered.

The lady who seemed frustrated with no help coming forth disclosed that she had contemplated taking out a loan, but does not know how she will payback.

Don Jazzy shortly after he was contacted by the lady sent her the sum of 1.2 million Naira which was the balance of the required money.

Some Nigerians in their reaction to the development praise Don Jazzy for always coming through when help is needed.
