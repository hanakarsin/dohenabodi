Ian Mbugua on the spotlight for criticising people

- Ian Mbugua, again, is getting trolled for his harsh role in Citizen TV's fashion segment Fashion Watch

- Larry Madowo asked him why he's a fashion judge while on theTrend and Kenyans were not happy with his answer

Ian Mbugua is known for his harshness.

Kenyans, however, are proving to be just as harsh on him.
