Jezelle Catherine - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok sensation known for her jezelle.x account, where she posts POV and acting videos. Her account has gained over 10 million followers and more than 314 million likes. She has also sold her own customized tees and hoodies on her jezellecatherine.com website. 

Before Fame

She posted her first TikTok in June 2020, gaining over 5,000 views. In July 2020, she posted a POV inspired by BbygShai that gained over 3 million views and more than 500,000 likes.

Trivia

Her account hit 1 million followers in March 2021. She's promoted brands including Pluto Case, GoodNight, and Cloudy. She has earned professional representation from Grail Talent. 

Family Life

She's from South Africa, and she occasionally uses Instagram to share photos of her pet dog. Her best friends are Skyjade and Valerie Leplech.

Associated With

She often duets videos from TikTok actors including Jason Van Tonder, Lindsay Kaine and Calvin Reef.
