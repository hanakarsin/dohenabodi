Heavily Pregnant Woman Allegedly Beaten To Death By Her Husband's Relative

The family of Betty Broderick, a 7-month-pregnant woman said to have been beaten to death by her husband’s relative, are demanding justice.

According to human rights activist, Israel Joe, the deceased, was heavily pregnant when she died after she was reportedly assaulted by the suspect identified as Jacob alias Tawho in Odimodi town, Burutu Local Government Area of Delta State.

Naija News understands that the incident occurred on December 17, 2021.

In a petition dated January 4, 2022 and addressed to Burutu Area Commander, the family said that the suspect attacked the deceased in the presence of her husband, Mr Samuel Broderick, following a heated argument that ensued between them on November 8, 2021.

Betty was rushed to Ogulaha hospital where she was confirmed to have lost her seven months pregnancy leading to some complications.
