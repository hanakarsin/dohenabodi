Kolanii - Trivia, Family, Bio

English YouTuber and gamer known for posting Minecraft content. His most popular videos include tips and tricks. He spent 100 Days collecting as many mobs as possible in Minecraft Hardcore. He has amassed more than 2.4 million subscribers on his Kolanii channel.

Before Fame

He joined YouTube in January 2020.

Trivia

One of his most viewed videos shows how he spent 100 days in One Block Minecraft. His goal was to defeat the ender dragon. Another popular video shows how he survived 100 Days in an ocean world.

Family Life

He is from England.

Associated With

He gained fame playing Minecraft, a game developed by Notch.
