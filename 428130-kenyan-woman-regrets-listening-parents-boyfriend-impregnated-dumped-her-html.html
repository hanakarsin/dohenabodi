Kenyan Woman Regrets Not Listening to Parents after Boyfriend Impregnated, Dumped Her

  • Miller Linda is a student pursuing Analytical Chemistry, but she deferred her studies in her second year
  • The 21-year-old has seen it all; in her pursuit for love, she has been heartbroken, impregnated and dumped, yet her family members know nothing of this
  • Linda is still nursing heartbreak after her boyfriend cheated on her with several women and isn't remorseful

When Miller Linda met her boyfriend last year, she trusted him with all her heart and never thought he would do anything to hurt her.

The 21-year-old recalled how her mom would always warn her about boys growing up, but she didn't think this included the man she was in love with.

While she was in school pursuing analytical chemistry, Linda met and fell in love with a man. The naive girl trusted him with all of her heart.

TUKO.co.ke reached out to Linda, who explained that it had been a challenging experience, especially because she had kept it from her parents.

While dealing with the infidelity heartbreak, Linda found out she was pregnant with her boyfriend's child.

She informed him about it, but he denied it saying he could not be the father.

"He asked me whose it was. I became so stressed about all of this. I even deferred school while in second year and my parents don't know about it yet. I eventually miscarried the pregnancy too," she told TUKO.co.ke.

However, Linda has decided to make good use of her painful experience and has started a project to support street children.

The project named Milan Street Life seeks to help street kids with basic needs like food, sanitary towels and love, to name the least.

The brilliant young girl told TUKO.co.ke that she plans on going back to school in January to finish her degree in analytical chemistry.

She also has a Youtube channel called Lindah Miller, where she hopes to encourage other young girls to live their lives cautiously.

"I wish I listened to my mom's advice, if I did, I would not have gone through all of this," she said.
