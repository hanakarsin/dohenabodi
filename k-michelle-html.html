K Michelle - Age, Family, Bio

R&B singer who charted singles with "Fakin' It" and "Can't Do This." She released her debut album in 2013 called Rebellious Soul.

Before Fame

She attended Florida A&M University on a music scholarship. She signed her first record contract with Jive Records in 2008.

Trivia

She was also a star on the reality series Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.

Family Life

In 2004, she welcomed a son. In 2016, she began dating Kastan Sims. She previously dated NBA shooting guard J.R. Smith. 

Associated With

Her 2012 single "Baby You" featured R. Kelly.
