News anchor Victoria Rubadiri emerges winner of presidential poll by Kenyan Twitter user

- Twitter user @jonhnjenga did a snap presidential election poll on Twitter pitting Mukhisa Kituyi, Jerotich Seii, Victoria Rubadiri and Hassan Ali Joho.

- A impressive number of 4, 575 tweeps took part in the poll

- Rubadiri emerged the winner with 38% of the vote, with Jerotich taking second place

Dr Lemayian Njenga Karugia J, @jonhnjenga, did a snap presidential election poll on Twitter to see who would be chosen by tweeps as the next president of Kenya.

Those involved in the online vote were Mukhisa Kituyi, Jerotich Seii, Victoria Rubadiri and Hassan Ali Joho.

A huge number of 4, 575 tweeps took part in the elections held on Sunday, February 21, and it had a pretty surprising outcome.

Rubadiri emerged the winner with 38% of the voters believing she would make a great president.

She was followed by Mukhisa Kituyi in second place with 28% of the voters choosing him as their president and Jerotich in the third position with 22%.

It was definitely a surprise to see Joho emerge as the biggest loser of the vote and even more astonishing is that he only got 12%.

This showed just how Kenyans are tired of the current leadership and might be going for fresh faces in the 2022 general election.

In the said poll, Jerotich ( a renowned human rights activist), and Rubadiri, got 60% of the combined vote which shows great trust, hopefully going forward, in women leadership.

If Rubadiri should consider making any political move, then one thing she will have to work hard on is her Swahili.

This after she and fellow news anchor Jeff Koinange were put to the test by Lulu Hassan, to try and speak Swahili and guess what? They failed terribly.

Rubadiri took the first shot. She was confident that this time around she was bound to knock the ball off the park.

However, the ball did not even move and Lulu burst into laughter as she heard her dear friend fumble with her words like a little girl.

Then the cheeky Swahili presenter called Jeff and gave him the same task. It was like a comedy show.

The two had the hardest time trying to string up a coherent sentence as Lulu stood beside them ready to laugh when they fell.
