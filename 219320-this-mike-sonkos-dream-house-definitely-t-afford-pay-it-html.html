This is Mike Sonko's dream house and you most definitely can't afford to pay for it

We all know that Nairobi Senator Mike Sonko is filthy rich.

We just don't know how rich he is or where he gets his riches from, which he always makes it obvious to everyone.

When he is not flaunting his gold rings and chains he is busy dishing out money to lucky Kenyans.

When his father Mike Sonko passed on in September 2015, Kenyans were shocked to see images of Sonko's home in Mua Hills in Maanzoni, Machakos.
