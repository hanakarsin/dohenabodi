Ebru Sahin - Age, Family, Bio

Turkish actress who has appeared in films such as Monolog and Piyasadan Büyük Alacagimiz Var. She has played the role of Vesile in the TV series Harem. She became widely known for her starring role as Reyyan Sadoglu on Hercai.

Before Fame

One of her earliest roles was as Zeynep in the 2003 TV show Hayat bilgisi. She didn't start acting until after graduating from Istanbul University.

Trivia

She has accrued over 4 million followers on her rebrusahin Instagram account. She became engaged to Cedi Osman in September 2021. In a short span, she earned multiple nominations for awards as an actress, and in 2020 won a Golden Palm Award for Best TV Series Actress.

Family Life

She posted a photo with her grandmother to her Instagram in May 2018. 

Associated With

She acted alongside Cansu Tosun in the TV show Küçük Hanimefendi.
